Bird Control

Birds cause significant damage to food grains, both in fields and storage. They consume 8-25 grams per day. They spoil grains, contaminate with excreta, feathers, and bodies, and spread diseases. Their noisy behavior and unhygienic conditions in warehouses and buildings necessitate bird control to reduce losses.

Termite Control

Anti-termite treatment is vital in construction and consists of pre-construction and post-construction phases. Pre-construction treatment follows Indian Standard Specification IS: 6313: 1981-1991 (Part II) and includes five stages, addressing various areas from trenches to the external perimeter. Post-construction treatment, as per IS: 6313: 1981-91 (Part III), involves drilling or trenching around the building, treating wooden structures, and applying sprays to prevent termite damage.

Dairy/ Poultry Pest Management

India, once known as "Sone ki chidiya" for its prosperity, has faced changes over time. However, challenges like nutritional standards, livestock care, disease control, genetic improvements, environmental issues, and pest control have emerged.
